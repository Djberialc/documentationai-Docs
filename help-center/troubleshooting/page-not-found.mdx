---
title: Page Not Found Error
description: What to do if a page doesn't load in your docs.
---

## Symptoms

- "404 Page Not Found" error
- Cannot access specific documentation

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the URL">
    Make sure the URL is correct and uses hyphens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Help Center">
    Visit the <a href="/help-center">Help Center home</a> and navigate again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Try reloading or clearing your browser cache.
  </Step>
</Steps>
