## Overview

Welcome to the Вячеслав Пономарев documentation space. You get a centralized hub to create, organize, and maintain all your project documentation. Build structured guides, API references, and changelogs with MDX-powered pages that render beautifully across devices.

This space supports version control, search, and collaboration features. Start by adding pages for quickstarts, authentication flows, and FAQs. Customize with your brand color `{ #3B82F6 }` for a professional look.

## Quick Start

Get your first documentation page live in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Page" icon="plus">
    Navigate to the dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com` and click "New Page". Choose the `introduction.mdx` template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Edit the YAML frontmatter with your `title` and `description`. Start body content with `## Overview`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & Publish" icon="eye">
    Preview changes in real-time. Publish to make it live for your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
